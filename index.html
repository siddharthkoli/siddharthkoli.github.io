Custom Text to Image Encoder/Decoder

This is a custom text to image encoder and decoder that I made over a weekend to learn some basics of opencv and text encoding.
There are 2 versions of it — Single character and Double character encoding/decoding. You can try it out over here: 2 Char and 1 Char

The idea stemmed from when I was sharing a Spotify link to a song, and it displayed a custom image that could only be decoded by the Spotify app. This led me to dive into how QR codes work and creating my own encoding algorithm.

CRONUS

This is an application to help track all the debt investments my family and I have made over the years. It keeps everything organized and sends out timely reminders for key events, like when an investment matures or when interest payments are due. It’s super helpful for keeping us on top of things without the need to manually check all the time.

The app is integrated with the Telegram Bot API, which means I can interact with it directly through Telegram. I can quickly poll for information or get notifications pushed straight to my phone on Telegram.

It’s designed with a microservice-based architecture in Spring Boot — because I wanted to learn how to design and develop applications with microservices. Unfortunately, I can't share the codebase just yet (privacy reasons), but a demo for the bot is in the works!
